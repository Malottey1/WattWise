About WattWise

WattWise is a smart energy management platform designed to help households and businesses monitor usage, get AI-powered recommendations, and reduce costs while protecting the environment.

Our solution collects energy usage data from smart meters or IoT sensors, predicts peak usage periods, and recommends energy-saving actions. We also suggest switching to renewable energy sources when feasible.
